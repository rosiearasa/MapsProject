ABOUT THE GAME

This interactive game was created by students who had the great privilege of attending Zoom University. This was done in order to give you the chance of experiencing ordinary life at Zoom University from move-in-day to graduation - that is if you make all the right choices of a good ZOOMIVERSITY student!

HOW TO PLAY

click the "Start Game" button to start your day at Zoom University

read each option CAREFULLY!

when you are sure of your next step, press on the button of your choice
